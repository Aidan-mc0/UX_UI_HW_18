About Me

I am a Toronto based UX/UI designer who enjoys crafting memorable user experiences. Like in my own life, I craft designs that reflect my style and flair and no doubt stand out from the crowd. I always challenge the norm and champion creativity. Together we can tackle any design challenge and develop a solution that reflects your needs and helps you to stand out from the crowd. Why be regular when you can be exceptional?

My UX Skills

User Research

Every good design solution relies on a strong foundation of good research. The research helps frame exactly what problem I need to solve. The insights I gather from my user research helps to inform my decisions every step of the way during my design process. In order to solve a problem you first have to understand exactly what it is that a user wants and then I can address those problems.

User Interface Design

If the interface is the way users interact with your website or mobile app, then it is increasingly important for me as a UI designer to design intuitive and engaging user interfaces. By extension, that means within the user interface, I always aim to craft a memorable user experience that positively represents the clients.

Web Development
